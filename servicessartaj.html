Services

1. Josephite Math Club-Vice President.

Josephite Math Club of St. Joseph Higher Secondary School, Dhaka is the first institution-based math club in Bangladesh. I served this premier club as a Vice President during 2018-2019. As a vice president, my duty was to plan different events & meet corporate personnel for sponsorship. I successfully organized 8 seminars, 1 National Level Mathematics Festival. Under our executive committee, the club won the title of “Best Math Club 2018” given by the Bangladesh Mathematical Olympiad Committee (BdMO). This club furnished my event operational & negotiation skills.

2. Summation Magazine-Editor.

The summation is the official publication of Josephite Math Club. As an editor, my duty was to collect writings from the students, review them & publish them. I was responsible for dealing with the press & distribution. I learned proofreading & caption writing working with the editorial panel.

3. Joypurhat Cyclists-Founding Crew Chief.

Joypurhat Cyclists is the very first cycling group of Joypurhat. I founded this group in 2015. This is basically a bicycle touring group. We organize regular rides, bike Friday, camping, Bijoy ride, Mass awareness campaign in our locality. We are working on spreading the message to the commoners, “Burn Fat, Not Oil”.

4. Bangladesh Scouts Joypurhat District-JOTA JOTI Station Controller.

Jamboree on the Air & Jamboree on the Internet is a virtual camping which is organized by the World Organization of the Scouting Movement (WOSM). I am assigned by the National Headquarters of Bangladesh Scouts to control Joypurhat District Jamboree on the Air & Internet station.
